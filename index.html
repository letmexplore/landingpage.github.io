About Me

As a Civil Engineer, I have a strong foundation in both the technical and creative aspects of building design and construction. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering from Aligarh Muslim University and a Master's degree in Architecture from European Open University. During my studies, I gained a thorough understanding of engineering principles such as structural analysis, materials science, and project management. I also developed my skills in architectural design, including drafting, 3D modeling, and building information modeling (BIM). In addition, I completed several internships with leading design firms, where I gained hands-on experience in all aspects of the design process, from initial concept development to construction documentation. In my professional roles, I have worked on a range of projects including commercial buildings, residential developments, and public infrastructure. I am skilled in using a variety of design software including AutoCAD, Revit, and SketchUp, and am comfortable working with project management software to manage timelines and budgets. In addition to my technical skills, I am highly creative and detail-oriented, with a strong eye for design and aesthetics. I am comfortable working with clients to develop their design vision and to create innovative solutions that meet their requirements.
